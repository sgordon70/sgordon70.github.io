DropQuest Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

DropQuest is designed to collect as little data as possible.

What we collect

  • Location data — used only to detect when you've arrived at a scavenger hunt stop while playing. This data stays on your device and is never sent to any server we control.
  • Camera photos — hint photos you capture while creating a quest. These are embedded directly in the quest file you choose to share (via AirDrop, Messages, etc.) and are never uploaded to any server we control.

What we don't collect

  • No accounts, no sign-in, no personal information
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is ever sold or shared with third parties

How quests are shared

Quests are sent directly between devices using AirDrop, Messages, or similar tools you choose. DropQuest has no backend server — we simply don't have access to your quest data, your photos, or your location history.

Changes to this policy

If DropQuest's data practices change in the future, this page will be updated to reflect that.
